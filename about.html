About Us

Discover our mission, vision, and dedication to nurturing future leaders through education and character development.

Welcome to Mangaldan National High School

Mangaldan National High School is committed to providing a nurturing environment where students excel academically and grow personally. Our dedicated staff, enriching programs, and supportive community make us a unique place for young minds to thrive.

Our Mission

Mission: Mangaldan National High School is a center of academic excellence and total development of individuals.

Our Vision

Vision: Mangaldan National High School shall produce graduates fully equipped with knowledge, skills, and values necessary to face the challenges of a changing world.

Our History

History: Established on February 23, 1945, Mangaldan National High School has been a pillar of academic excellence in the community. We are proud of our heritage and excited about our future, always striving to provide the highest quality education.
