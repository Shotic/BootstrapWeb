Academic Tracks

Explore our diverse academic programs designed to prepare students for their future careers through a rich blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM)

The Accountancy, Business, and Management track is meticulously crafted to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of key business principles. This program encompasses essential subjects such as accounting, finance, marketing, and management, equipping students with the necessary knowledge and skills to pursue a successful career in the ever-evolving business landscape. Students in this track will explore various facets of entrepreneurship, making it an ideal choice for those aspiring to establish their own ventures or to excel in corporate environments.

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)

The STEM track offers a rigorous curriculum that emphasizes advanced concepts in the fields of science and mathematics. Students in this program will engage deeply with complex theories and hands-on experiments, preparing them for rewarding careers in engineering, technology, and various scientific disciplines. This track is ideal for those who have a passion for problem-solving and innovation, providing a solid foundation for further education in higher learning institutions or direct entry into the workforce in high-demand fields.

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

The Humanities and Social Sciences track centers around subjects that delve into human behavior, society, and culture. This program encompasses a variety of disciplines including literature, sociology, and psychology, making it an excellent choice for students interested in understanding the complexities of the human experience. Those pursuing this track will be well-prepared for careers in fields such as education, law, psychology, social work, and communication, where they can apply their knowledge to make a meaningful impact in their communities.

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL)

The Technical-Vocational-Livelihood track is designed to provide students with practical skills and training tailored for various vocational careers. This program emphasizes hands-on experiences and real-world applications, ensuring that students are job-ready upon graduation. Participants will have the opportunity to explore multiple fields, acquiring valuable competencies that can lead to immediate employment or further educational pursuits in technical disciplines. This track is perfect for those looking to gain specific vocational skills while still in high school.

Information and Communications Technology (ICT)

The Information and Communications Technology track prepares students for dynamic careers in computing, programming, and digital media. This program focuses on developing practical skills in various technological areas, including software development, network management, and multimedia production. Students will learn to navigate the digital landscape effectively, preparing them for high-demand roles in a technology-driven world. This track is ideal for those who have a strong interest in technology and want to harness its potential for innovation and problem-solving.

SMAW (Shielded Metal Arc Welding)

The SMAW (Shielded Metal Arc Welding) program focuses on developing essential skills in various welding techniques, particularly using electric arcs to join metals. This hands-on training emphasizes safety practices, precision, and the ability to work with a variety of materials in multiple positions. Students will gain practical experience that prepares them for immediate employment in the manufacturing and construction sectors, where skilled welders are in high demand. This track is ideal for individuals looking to pursue a career in the skilled trades.

Cookery

The Cookery program offers a comprehensive exploration of culinary arts, including the preparation, cooking, and presentation of a diverse array of dishes. Students will learn essential cooking techniques, ingredient selection, and nutritional considerations, enabling them to create flavorful and health-conscious meals. This track not only cultivates practical cooking skills but also nurtures creativity in the kitchen, preparing students for careers in hospitality, catering, and other culinary fields. It is perfect for aspiring chefs and culinary enthusiasts alike.

IEM (Industrial Engineering and Management)

The Industrial Engineering and Management track equips students with vital skills needed to optimize processes and enhance efficiency across various industries. This comprehensive program covers essential topics such as production management, systems design, quality control, and operational research, preparing students for diverse roles in engineering and business management. Graduates will be well-prepared to tackle challenges in modern workplaces, making this track ideal for those who aspire to lead and innovate in their future careers.

Junior High School Programs

Special Science Class (SSC)

The Special Science Class (SSC) is a unique program tailored for students with a strong interest in science and mathematics. This track offers an advanced curriculum, focusing on subjects like biology, chemistry, and physics, complemented by research projects and laboratory work. It aims to cultivate critical thinking and analytical skills, providing a solid foundation for students aspiring to pursue degrees in scientific fields or medicine.

Special Program in Journalism (SPJ)

The Special Program in Journalism (SPJ) is designed for aspiring journalists and communicators. This track provides intensive training in writing, reporting, and multimedia production. Students will engage in real-world projects, honing their storytelling skills while exploring the ethical and professional standards of journalism. This program is ideal for those looking to make a significant impact in the field of media and communication.

Special Program in Math (SPM)

The Special Program in Math (SPM) is aimed at students who have a keen interest in mathematics and its applications. This track offers a deeper exploration of mathematical concepts and problem-solving techniques, preparing students for careers in fields such as engineering, finance, and data science. The program emphasizes critical thinking and analytical skills, ensuring that graduates are well-equipped for advanced studies or professional challenges in quantitative disciplines.

Special Program in Arts (SPA)

The Special Program in Arts (SPA) caters to creative students passionate about various art forms, including visual arts, performing arts, and music. This track encourages artistic expression and critical appreciation of the arts through practical and theoretical learning. Students will develop their talents while gaining insight into the cultural significance of the arts, preparing them for careers in creative industries or further studies in the field of arts and design.

Special Program in Foreign Languages (SPFL)

The Special Program in Foreign Languages (SPFL) focuses on language acquisition and cultural understanding. This track offers students the opportunity to learn multiple foreign languages, enhancing their communication skills and global perspective. With a strong emphasis on immersive learning experiences, students will engage with native speakers and explore the cultural contexts of the languages they study, preparing them for careers in international relations, translation, and other global fields.

Special Program in Sports (SPS)

The Special Program in Sports (SPS) is designed for students interested in athletic training and sports management. This track offers a comprehensive approach to physical education, covering topics such as coaching, sports science, and health education. With a focus on developing leadership skills and teamwork, this program prepares students for careers in sports coaching, physical therapy, and sports administration.
